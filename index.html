About

I'm Stephanie, motion designer, illustrator, and creative director based in London. I've spent years building visual systems for apps, brands, and campaigns that actually make people feel something.

Growing up across five countries gave me something you can't learn in a classroom: the ability to read a room, adapt, and find what connects. That shows up in the work.

Alongside client work I've started building my own things. Digital products designed and coded from the ground up. I'm curious about where creativity and technology meet, and I like working at that edge.

When I'm not designing I'm probably in a coffee shop, deep in a philosophy book, or convinced that one more iteration will make it perfect.
